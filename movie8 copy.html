The Incredibles
Director Brad Birds
Leading Actor Craig T. Nelson
Leading Actress Holly Hunter
Rating G
In an era of superheroes saving city's across the world, superheroes Mr. Incredible and Elastagirl settle down and have a family, as society decides to shun every vigilante in its new government attempt to disassociate. Years later, as their children develop powers, The Incredibles family run into difficulties as former enemies attempt to wreak havoc in the current superhero ridden society.
